Plane Jumpers

Chase rings through a sunlit skyline in this aero-runner inspired by Subway Surfers. The latest build delivers camera-swayed perspective, a high-altitude runway, and a sculpted stunt plane that banks, trails, and glows like it just cleared a cloud stack.

Upgraded feel

• Camera sway tracks every lane change for a fuller 3D illusion.
• Parallax skyscrapers, contrailing traffic, and a glowing runway ladder your depth cues.
• The hero jet is redrawn with metallic shading, afterburn pulses, and procedural exhaust.

Systems & scoring

• Freighters join drones and turbulence pods for multi-size dodging.
• HUD now tracks combo chains, altitude percentage, and indicated airspeed.
• Difficulty presets and RNG seeding let you script practice runs or reproducible captures.
• Jet trails and runway strips are all procedural — tweak anything without external art.

How to launch

  • Install pygame (3.9+ recommended):
    python -m pip install pygame
  • Play in a window:
    python plane_jumpers.py
  • CI / headless capture:
    SDL_VIDEODRIVER=dummy python plane_jumpers.py --headless --frames 600 --screenshot artifacts/plane_jumpers.png
  • Lock a practice seed or change pacing:
    python plane_jumpers.py --difficulty ace --seed 2024

Controls

  • Left / Right (or A / D) — bank across the three air lanes
  • Space / W / Up Arrow — climb to clear cargo freighters and drones
  • Esc / Q — exit instantly
  • Enter — restart after a crash landing
Everything is still procedurally drawn inside plane_jumpers.py. Adjust skyline palettes, runway geometry, obstacle cadence, or HUD details to suit your vibe — no spritesheets required.
